DATASET ANALYSIS NOTES
======================
Dataset: Online Gaming Behaviour Dataset
Source: Kaggle
Total Records: 9000+
Total Columns: 13

Key Observations:
- 60% of players are Male, 40% Female
- Most players are aged 15-49
- Top game genres: Sports, Action, RPG, Strategy, Simulation
- Play time ranges from 0 to 24 hours
- Locations: USA (40%), Europe (30%), Asia and Others (30%)

Simulation Assumptions Based on Dataset:
- Server capacity set to 8 (based on average session data)
- Player arrival rate modeled on sessions per week data
- Wait times reflect real engagement patterns
